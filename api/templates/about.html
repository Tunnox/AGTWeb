The AGT Web App is a secure digital platform developed for Amazing Grace Tabernacle, Sheffield. It helps members, leaders, and administrators collect, store, and access church records in a structured, responsible, and transparent way.

  • Connect with each other
  • Share information safely
  • Manage church activities efficiently
  • Ensure data is handled fairly and securely

For Members

  • Update and manage personal information
  • Register attendance at services and events
  • Access sermons, teaching materials, and resources
  • Submit prayer requests or join community groups
  • Receive secure announcements and communications

For Leaders & Administrators

  • Manage membership databases in a secure, centralized system
  • Schedule and track events and activities
  • Oversee donations and giving records
  • Communicate safely with members and leaders
  • Maintain accountability through transparent data management
  • Strengthens connections between members, leaders, and groups
  • Ensures compliance with UK GDPR regulations
  • Handles data responsibly, with consent and transparency
  • Protects information through encryption, access controls, and audits
  • Builds trust through responsible stewardship of personal and financial data
  • UK GDPR Compliance: Personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently
  • Consent & Stewardship: Members are informed about how their data is used
  • Role-Based Access: Only authorized personnel can access sensitive records
  • Accountability: Data is managed under the oversight of the AGT Admin Centre
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Keep your personal details up to date
  • Never share your login credentials
  • Report suspicious or incorrect data immediately
  • Communicate respectfully and with integrity
  • All records are securely encrypted and backed up
  • Access is controlled through roles: Admin, Leader, Member, Guest
  • Regular audits and monitoring ensure compliance
  • Rapid response procedures are in place for errors or breaches
